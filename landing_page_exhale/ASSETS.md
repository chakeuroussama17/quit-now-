# Exhale landing — images to add

Drop these into `pictures/` with the **exact filenames** below. Until you do,
each slot shows a labelled dashed placeholder on the page telling you what goes
there — nothing breaks, so you can publish and fill them in over time.

The app screenshots are the most important. Take them on a phone or emulator in
**dark mode**, portrait, then crop to the phone (9:19.5). PNG preferred.

| File | What it is | How to get it |
|---|---|---|
| `app_icon.png` | Exhale logo, square, transparent bg | Export from `assets/images/icon.png` in the app repo (the teal breath mark) |
| `favicon.png` | Browser tab icon (same mark, ~64px) | Same as above, small |
| `home.png` | **Home screen** — streak counter, money saved, next milestone | Screenshot the app Home tab (dark mode) |
| `log.png` | **Log sheet** — product + trigger + emotion chips + intensity slider | Screenshot the "+" log sheet |
| `stats.png` | **Stats** — hour×weekday heatmap + trigger breakdown | Screenshot the Stats tab |
| `sos.png` | **Craving SOS** — breathing circle + timer + "I beat it" button | Screenshot the SOS tab |
| `google_play.png` | Official "Get it on Google Play" badge | Download from Google's brand page (play.google.com/intl/en_us/badges) |
| `og_image.png` | Social-share preview (1200×630) — optional | Make one when convenient (logo + tagline on dark bg) |

## If you'd rather generate the screenshots with AI

Use this prompt, then swap the wording per screen:

> *A single mobile app screen mockup, dark UI (#0A0A0C background, #141418 cards),
> one mint-teal accent (#4ADEB5), Inter font, generous spacing, premium fitness-app
> aesthetic like Whoop or Strava. No clutter. Portrait 9:19.5. Screen: [DESCRIBE].*

- **home.png** → *a big live "9 days smoke-free" counter, a "Money saved RM 142" tile, and a "Next: day 14 — Circulation improving" row*
- **log.png** → *a bottom sheet titled "Log a smoke" with chip groups (Stress, Habit, Coffee…), an emotion row, and a teal intensity slider, plus a teal "Log it" button*
- **stats.png** → *a heatmap grid of teal cells (hour of day × day of week) and a horizontal bar breakdown of triggers*
- **sos.png** → *a large glowing teal breathing circle reading "Breathe", a 4:23 timer below, quick-action buttons, and a teal "I beat it — log this win" button*

## Notes
- The `og_image.png` and `favicon.png` are optional niceties — the page looks
  complete without them.
- The Google Play badge is a real downloadable asset from Google; don't recreate
  it yourself (brand guidelines). Point its link at your Play Store listing once live.
