Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Exhale (“the app”, “we”, “us”) helps you quit smoking and vaping. We built it privacy-first: your most sensitive information — every smoke and craving log, your statistics, and your “The Room” support conversations — never leaves your device. This policy explains the limited data we do handle, why, and the choices you have.

The short version. We store your account (email), your profile, and your subscription status so the app works across sign-ins. Your behavioural logs and private chats stay only on your phone. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time, from inside the app.

1. Data that stays only on your device

The following are stored locally in an on-device database and are never uploaded to us:

  • Your smoke and vape logs, cravings, triggers, moods and notes
  • Your statistics, streaks, milestones and achievements
  • Your conversations in “The Room” (the private emotional-support chat)
  • App settings, reminders and language preference

If you delete the app, this data is erased with it. You can also wipe it any time in Settings → Delete all data.

2. Data we store in the cloud (Supabase)

To give you an account that works across devices and sign-ins, we store, tied to your user ID:

DataWhy
Email address (and Google account ID if you sign in with Google)To create and secure your account
Profile: display name, chosen products, quit date, quit reason, reward goal, currencyTo personalise the app and sync your setup
Profile photo (optional, if you add one)Shown as your avatar
Subscription statusTo unlock premium features you’ve paid for

Community (optional)

If you join the public country chat rooms, the nickname you choose, your country, and the messages you post are stored and shown publicly to other users. Do not post anything you wish to keep private. You can report or block other users; we store those actions to keep the rooms safe.

3. AI coach

When you use the AI coaching or SOS features, the messages you send — together with limited, non-identifying context about your progress (such as your streak length and current craving) — are sent through our backend to OpenAI to generate a reply. We do not send your email, your logs history, or anything from “The Room”. If AI is unavailable, the app falls back to built-in offline guidance.

4. Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) and managed through RevenueCat, our subscription provider. We never see or store your payment card details — Apple handles the transaction. We receive only your subscription status (active or not).

5. Notifications & photos

  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device for reminders and milestones. They are not sent from our servers and require your permission.
  • Photo library access is requested only if you choose to set a profile picture, and only to let you pick that one image.

6. Feedback

If you send feedback from Settings, we store your message and category, plus basic diagnostics (app version, operating system, and language) to help us fix issues. Nothing from your logs or “The Room” is included.

7. Who we share data with

We use these processors purely to run the service. We do not sell your data to anyone.

  • Supabase — account, profile and community data hosting
  • OpenAI — generating AI coach replies
  • RevenueCat & Apple — subscription processing
  • Resend — delivering feedback emails to us

8. Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account at any time in Settings → Account → Delete account. This immediately and irreversibly deletes your account, profile, subscription record, community profile and messages from our servers, and erases all data on your device. If you need help, email us at the address below and we will delete your data on request.

9. Children

Exhale is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; we’ll revise the “Last updated” date above when we do.
